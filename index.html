About

From interpreter to data reader

I graduated with a degree in International Business Administration from Providence University, Taichung (GPA 3.7/4.3). Throughout my four years in Taiwan, I worked as an interpreter for Taiwan's Ministry of Education (MOE) programs and served as a teaching/event assistant for over 30 academic events.

I also spent time as a Business Development & Telesales intern at a logistics startup, generating 20+ leads in my first two months. That role has since ended — while it taught me a lot about outreach and client communication, logistics isn't the path I plan to go deeper into.

"My real strength isn't tied to one industry — it's the ability to connect: between people, and now, between data and decisions."

I'm now preparing to begin an MBA program at NTUT (National Taipei University of Technology) in September 2026, building toward a Data/Business Analyst path that combines a stronger data foundation with my cross-cultural communication background.
